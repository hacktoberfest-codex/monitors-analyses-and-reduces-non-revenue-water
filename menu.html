Web Application

Drop BY Drop is the web Application which help users in Monitoring their water uses and provides an easy payment method with tips on water conservation.

Overview of Non-Revenue Water

Non-revenue water (NRW) refers to the portion of treated water lost before reaching paying customers due to leaks, theft, or inaccurate metering.

Responsive design

In this website users can make necessary ammendments in their respective profiles as per the requirements and accordingly pay for the house water load.

Leak Detection and Repair

We comapre the water limit of the user with their consumption and if there is a large difference we send them alerts and ask them to check for leakages and provide solutions for the same.

Data Analysis and Reporting

By comparing our data with government's data we report the authorities about the unauthorized water thefting or any leakages happening in the area.

Metering and Monitoring

By Monitoring and providing link between government data through water meters and our website's calculation we help the government to detect the reasons for the NRW.
