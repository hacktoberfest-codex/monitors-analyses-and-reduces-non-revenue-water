OUR SERVICES

Revenue By Location

After signin in our website, service providers of different localities can see the revenue generated in their respective areas.

It also enables the service providers to differentiate between wealthy and mediocre societies.

Current Week And Previous Week Revenue

After Logging in Service providers can compare their revenue generated in the present week with the revenue generated in the previous week.

Thereby helps them in monitoring the cause for lower revenue generated like leakages and unauthorized thefting.

Alerts for Leakage Detection

The service provider can set the limit for NRW and according to the amount of water they supplied and revenue generated if the calculated limit exceeds then we send alerts to the service provider for leakage detection

Thereby helps them in lowering the NRW.
