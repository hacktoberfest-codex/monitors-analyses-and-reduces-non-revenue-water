Why is NRW important?

When a significant amount of water is lost, it represents a loss of revenue for the utility. High NRW means that more water needs to be extracted, treated, and distributed, which can strain water resources and energy supplies.

What causes NRW?

Leakage, Burst Pipes, Water Theft, Metering and Billing Errors Unauthorized Consumption Data Handling Errors

What are the common methods for measuring NRW ?

Methods for measuring NRW are by installing flow meters at strategic points,Pressure Management,Acoustic Leak Detection.

How can I report water leaks or suspected theft in my area?

Residents can report water-related issues to their local water utility or authorities and also select this reason for water loss.
