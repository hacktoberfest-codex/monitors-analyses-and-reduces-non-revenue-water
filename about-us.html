Our Vision

At Drop By Drop, we envision a world where water is cherished, conserved, and sustainably managed for the benefit of all living beings and our planet. We believe that by raising awareness and providing practical tools, we can contribute to a more water-conscious society.

What We Do

Our app allows you to easily track your daily water usage, helping you become more mindful of your consumption habits which takes meaningful steps towards reducing waste. Stay informed about the quality of your local water supply. We provide real-time updates and alerts, ensuring you have access to safe and clean water.
